RN Financial Research Centre rnfc

4568B1105

This course is designed to learn about how R and C++ languages work and how students can use them in the development, testing, and deployment of data pre-processing methods, mathematical modelling and computational techniques for creating predictive models.

Focus

  • Computational Finance - Algorithmic trading models, and tools for data management, modelling interfaces, and performance analysis.
  • Computational Marketing - Exploiting heterogeneity to reveal consumer preference.
  • Computational Biology: DNA sequencing methods, and bioinformatics to sequence, assemble, and analyze the function and structure of genomes.

In this course we offer a rapid prototyping environment that provides a reliable and interoperable platform for big data analytics, data mining, and data science education at the Ivey School of Business.

4517B3105

This course provides students with a unique real-world leveraged finance education. The emphasis of this course is on High Yield Credit models and Utility Infrastructure modelling with Excel and VBA. The course offers an unique opportunity for HBA students to learn advanced technical / quantitative skills while learning how to properly set-up a series of complex financial models.
